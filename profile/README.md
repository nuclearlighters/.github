## What is MuleCube?

A Raspberry Pi 5 that creates its own WiFi network and serves Wikipedia, maps, AI chat, emergency communications, and 30+ other services — no internet required. Built for sailors, expedition teams, emergency responders, and anyone who needs reliable information off-grid.
